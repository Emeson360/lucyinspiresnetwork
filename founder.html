About Us

Meet the Founder

Lucy Ebere Okafor is Executive Director of several non-profit organizations in southeastern Nigeria. She is a driven and visionary leader who is dedicated to youth discovery and gender empowerment.

In 2016 she founded the Lucy Inspires Network and Hadassah School of Leadership, both of which are renowned for their commitment to youth development and women .

Recently, Lucy launched the ROBW (Real Online Business Women) community, a platform that provides mentorship and resources to women seeking financial independence and success. She accomplishes this through her digital marketing platform called Enugubizwomen.

Lucy is also an accomplished author, speaker, and brand influencer, and an advocate for girl child education. She aspires to touch the lives of thousands of youths and women over the next five years, providing them with the proper mentorship and education they need to achieve greatness.

Lucy is happily married to a wonderful husband, and they are blessed with amazing children.

Our Vision

Our vision is to cultivate purpose-driven, well-prepared, self-sufficient, and transformative young people and women who are essential to the advancement of their nation.

Our vision extends to empowering these individuals with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to make a meaningful impact in their communities and beyond. We strive to nurture a generation of trailblazers who will break through societal barriers, inspire positive change, and contribute to a more equitable and prosperous future for all.

Here are some of our Outlined Programs/Events

1. Girls Club:

This distinctive program is established across various communities, providing a space for girls aged 12-18 to engage in self-discovery, reading techniques, vocational skills, public speaking, and overall excellence. Through this initiative, we create a supportive and nurturing environment that encourages personal growth and fosters confidence in these young girls. By offering a well-rounded curriculum, we equip them with essential life skills and knowledge that will empower them to pursue their ambitions and contribute positively to their communities and the world at large.

2. Hadassah School of Leadership and Values:

Through the Hadassah School of Leadership and Values, Lucy Inspires Network connects with young girls from underprivileged communities to integrate them into our enduring learning and mentorship programs. The primary objective of this leadership school is to cultivate outstanding female leaders who embody strong values and principles.

By offering these girls access to valuable resources, guidance, and support, we aim to foster an environment that nurtures their potential and helps them overcome the challenges they face. Through tailored educational experiences and personal development opportunities, we strive to instil in them the necessary skills and confidence to become agents of change and role models for future generations.

3. Business Hangout /Summit for Women:

Lucy Inspires Network, utilizing its online digital marketing platform (Enugubizwomen), has coordinated outdoor business gatherings for women. This platform offers women the opportunity to promote their businesses online at significantly reduced rates, further supporting their entrepreneurial endeavours.

These outdoor business events provide a valuable networking space for women entrepreneurs, allowing them to connect, share experiences, and collaborate with one another. By fostering a community of like-minded individuals, Lucy Inspires Network empowers women to grow their businesses, gain new insights, and access resources that can contribute to their long-term success.

4. Her Platform Conference:

The annual platform conference, held in conjunction with International Women's Day, aims to motivate women to expand their horizons, take on greater challenges, and become problem solvers for their communities, nations, and the world as a whole. This event also serves as an opportunity to honour and celebrate women who have achieved remarkable success in their diverse fields of expertise.

During this event, participants have the chance to hear from inspiring speakers, attend workshops, and engage in interactive sessions that foster personal growth and professional development. By creating a supportive environment for women to learn from one another and share their experiences, Lucy Inspires Network aims to empower women to break through barriers, pursue their dreams, and make a lasting impact on society.

VOLUNTEERS

At Lucy inspires network, we believe that our volunteers are the lifeblood of our organization. Their dedication, passion, and commitment to our cause are the driving forces behind the positive impact we make in the lives of those we serve. By generously donating their time, skills, and talents, our volunteers contribute to the growth and success of our mission, helping us create lasting change in our community and beyond.

We are incredibly grateful for our diverse team of volunteers, who come from all walks of life, each bringing unique perspectives and experiences to our organization. Whether they are assisting with fundraising events, offering professional expertise, or providing hands-on support to our programs, our volunteers are essential in helping us achieve our goals.

As part of our commitment to supporting our volunteers, we strive to create an inclusive, engaging, and nurturing environment where they can grow personally and professionally. We offer various opportunities for volunteers to develop new skills, network with like-minded individuals, and gain a deeper understanding of the issues we address.

We are always eager to welcome new volunteers to join our growing family. No matter your background, interests, or availability, there is a place for you at Lucy inspires network. By volunteering with us, you will not only make a difference in the lives of those in need, but you will also become part of a compassionate and dedicated community that shares your desire to create a better world.
